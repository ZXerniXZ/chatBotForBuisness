Pet Policy

Dogs and other pets are welcome in our outdoor seating area only.

**Pet Guidelines:**
- Dogs must be on a leash at all times
- Well-behaved dogs only
- Please clean up after your pet
- Service animals are welcome inside the restaurant
- Maximum 2 pets per table

**Outdoor Seating:**
- Available during warm weather (April-October)
- Covered patio area
- Water bowls provided for pets
- Pet-friendly staff

**Service Animals:**
- Service animals are always welcome
- No restrictions on service animals
- Please inform staff if you have a service animal
